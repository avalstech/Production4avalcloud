Frequently asked questions

Is AvalCloud a replacement for AWS?
No. AvalCloud runs on top of AWS. You get the same underlying infrastructure (EC2, S3, RDS, Route 53 and more) but with simpler plans, friendlier pricing and a hosting-style dashboard.
Can I move existing sites from another host?
Yes. Shared hosting plans include free migrations for typical PHP sites and WordPress. For more complex stacks, AvalCloud‚Äôs support team or partners can guide you through a manual migration.
Where are my sites hosted physically?
Your resources live in AWS data centers. At signup you choose a preferred region (for example, Europe, US or Africa once enabled) so you can keep latency low for your main audience.
Do I need an AWS account to use AvalCloud?
No. AvalCloud manages AWS accounts and infrastructure for you. You just pay the AvalCloud subscription. Later, advanced users will be able to connect their own AWS account if preferred.
How will billing work?
Initially, plans are billed monthly in USD with flat pricing for shared hosting and VPS tiers. As the product evolves you can plug in multiple payment providers and local currencies.
Can I resell AvalCloud to my clients?
Yes. Reseller plans let you package hosting under your own brand while AvalCloud takes care of AWS, uptime and security in the background.
